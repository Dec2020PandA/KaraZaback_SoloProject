SOS - Save Our Shore

Due to the rise of pollution in oceans there is an immediate need for cleanups around the world. Starting with getting young teens involved, Save Our Shore (SOS) focuses on conserving the planet by implementing beach cleanups that rid beaches of trash that is harmful to sea life and the ocean itself.
